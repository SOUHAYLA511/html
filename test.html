Who are BTS ?

BTS is a South Korean boy band formed in 2013. The seven members are RM, Jin, Suga, J-Hope, Jimin, V, and Jungkook. They are known for their mix of pop and hip-hop, addressing social and personal issues in their music, and have achieved global fame.
